Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At GradientCast, we believe that your weather data should be as clear as the sky on a sunny day. Your privacy is a core part of our philosophy.

1. No Personal Data Collection

GradientCast does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We do not require account registration, and we do not collect your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Location and Weather Requests

When you search for a city, the name of that city is sent to our weather data provider to retrieve the current forecast. This data is processed in real-time and is not linked to you personally. We do not track your location in the background.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party APIs to provide accurate weather information. These services receive only the minimum information necessary to provide the forecast (such as city name or coordinates). They do not receive any data that identifies you.

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our application.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our support page.
